Decision Review System

The Decision Review System, DRS, a game-changer in cricket's fold, A tool to challenge umpiring decisions, stories oft retold, Using technology like ball tracking and hot spot, To aid in getting crucial calls right, whether cold or hot.

LBW

LBW, or Leg Before Wicket, a rule quite profound, When the ball strikes the batsman's leg, confusion may abound, Umpires judge if it would have hit the stumps, a complex decree, Determining the fate of the batsman, in cricket's legal spree.
